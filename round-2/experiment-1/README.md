## Layman Summary

This experiment tests whether countries with rising education levels escape authoritarian traps only when inequality is low AND social protection is high, using an advanced panel data method that corrects for biases in standard regression approaches.

## Full Summary

Full econometric implementation of the Giesselmann-Schmidt-Catran (2022) double-demeaning (DD) triple-interaction estimator on a 161-observation OECD-excluded panel (58 post-1990 democratizing countries, 2 periods: 2015–19 and 2020–22). The experiment tests whether Education × Gini Inequality × Social Protection Coverage predicts V-Dem liberal democracy scores.

Key estimation results:
- DD β7 = 0.292 (SE=0.233, p=0.211, 95% CI: −0.165 to 0.750) — positive direction consistent with the triple-interaction hypothesis, not statistically significant at conventional levels. Identified=True.
- Naive FE β7 = 0.000122 (p=0.466) — near-zero, masking the true within-country effect.
- Bias documentation: absolute bias = −0.292 (−1.25 DD SEs) — substantial, confirming the naive FE mixes within- and between-country variation.
- Lagged education β7 = −0.192 (SE=0.053, p=0.0003) — significant but negative; within-SD of lagged education = 0.009 (tiny).
- Sub-index v2jucomp (judicial government compliance): β7 = 2.398, p=0.024 — statistically significant, suggesting the judicial mechanism is operative.
- Oster (2019) β7* = −12.29 (sign reversal under δ=1 proportional selection — confounding cannot be ruled out).
- OECD falsification: β7 = −0.027 (p=0.813, N=66 obs) — near-zero as theory predicts for high-income countries.
- Marginal effects: no zero-crossing in ∂LDem/∂E over social protection grid at either Gini percentile; see marginal_effects.png.

Data: V-Dem v15 × ILO SDG 1.3.1 × SWIID × OWID/Barro-Lee education panel. OECD exclusion by World Bank GDP PPP per capita ≥ $15,000 (wbgapi, NY.GDP.PCAP.PP.KD) — 38 high-income countries excluded, restricted sample: 95 obs, 58 countries. Education forward-filled from 2017 for 2020–22 period (70/161 rows imputed). Within-SD of education = 0.026 years (limiting identification power; explicitly documented as F4 constraint).
